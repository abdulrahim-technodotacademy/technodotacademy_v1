About Tomorrow Academy

Tomorrow Academy (Technodot Academy) is a leading provider of professional training, education, and career development services. Founded with a vision to bridge the gap between academic learning and industry requirements, we have empowered thousands of individuals and organizations to achieve their goals.

Our comprehensive ecosystem includes vendor certification programs, university degree courses, corporate training solutions, and career placement services. We partner with industry leaders, educational institutions, and corporations to deliver world-class learning experiences that transform careers and businesses.

With a presence across multiple countries and a team of expert trainers, we continue to innovate and expand our offerings to meet the evolving needs of the modern workforce.

Our Journey

2018

Foundation

Tomorrow Academy was founded with a vision to revolutionize professional education. Started with 2 training programs and 50 students.

2019

Expansion

Launched vendor certification programs in partnership with Zoho, Odoo, and Microsoft. Reached 500+ students across 3 cities.

2020

Digital Transformation

Adapted to remote learning during pandemic. Launched online degree programs in partnership with universities. Trained 2000+ students online.

2021

Corporate Solutions

Launched business solutions division offering corporate training and consulting. Onboarded 100+ corporate clients.

2022

Earn & Learn

Introduced innovative Earn & Learn program providing stipends to students. Placed 500+ students in paid internships.

2023

International Presence

Expanded operations to UAE, KSA, and Oman. Established partnerships with global companies and institutions.

2024

Continued Growth

Reached 50,000+ trained students, 500+ corporate partners, and presence in 15+ countries. Launched AI-powered learning platform.
